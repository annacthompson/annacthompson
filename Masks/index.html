Face Masks

Hello friends, family, and friends of friends! We could all probably use a new mask right about now!


This page displays face masks that I have made over the last several months and would love to send out into the world. They are made with two layers of fabric and include an adjustable over-the-head elastic. I have been wearing mine for months and find the shape and elastic to be secure and comfortable. I have been able to wash them and like that I can wear the elastic around my neck and over or under my hair. I've had positive feedback from many others as well!



What you need to know:

  • There are 3 sizes: Large (men and women), Medium (women and teenagers), and Small (children). For reference, I wear a medium.
  • All masks come with over-the-head elastic, whether it is pictured or not.
  • Some have nose wires. I can add a nose wire to any mask for $3.
  • I am happy to take requests and answer questions.
  • Pay via cash, check or Venmo.
  • Shipping is free.

Disclaimer: Are they perfect? No. Will anyone notice? Probably not. Any blemishes, like loose thread, are hidden on the inside. And of course, I can make no promises or guarantees about their effectiveness in a health setting. Use at your own risk.
